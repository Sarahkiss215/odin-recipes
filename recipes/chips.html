Chips

Description

Homemade chips are so delicious. They are very simple to make. All one need is potatoes, oil and a source of heat. They can be baked of fried. Ofcourse the baked chips are healthier but doesn't have the crispness.

We will be making fried chips. Good food for a healthy body. Let's not waste time!Let's cook!!

Ingredients

  • 500g potatoes
  • 500ml vegetable oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Pill, wash and slice the potatoes.
  2. Fill the saucepan 3/4 with vegetable oil and heat until bubbling.
  3. Fry the chips in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Remove the chips from oil using a spotted spoon and place on a kitchen roll to absorb the leftover oil.
  5. Sprinkle the chips with salt tand serve.
