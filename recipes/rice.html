Rice

Description

Rice is the seed of the grass species Oryza sativa (Asian rice) or less commonly O. glaberrima (African rice). As a cereal grain, domesticated rice is the most widely consumed staple food for over half of the world's human population. Cooking rice is simple and fun.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cupsof water
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 2 table spoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Wash and slice the onion and tomatoes.
  2. Place the sliced onions in a cooking pot.
  3. Add the vegetable oil and fry the onions until golden brown.
  4. Add sliced garlic and mix for a minute.
  5. Add the tomatoes, mix until a paste is formed.
  6. Wash rice and add it to the pot.
  7. Mix and then add water and salt, then boil it until all the water is no more.
  8. Serve while hot or warm.
